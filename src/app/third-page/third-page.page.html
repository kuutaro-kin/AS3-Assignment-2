Fettucine Alfredo

Author: Sam & Dan, Ahead of Thyme

Servings

3-4 plates
Prep Time

15 mins
Cook Time

20 mins

Fettuccine Alfredo is a classic Italian pasta dish made with fettuccine noodles, butter, cream, and Parmesan cheese. It is a rich and creamy dish that is perfect for a special occasion or a comforting meal. Fettuccine.... mmm yummy. Fun story, a friend of mine thought fettuccine was the term for those people who loves BLB. I was like, "No, fettucine is a pasta dish..."

Ingredients

  • 8 oz fettuccine pasta
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1/4 teaspoon nutmeg
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley
